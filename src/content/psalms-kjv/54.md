<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Behold, God <em>is</em> mine helper: the Lord <em>is</em> with them that uphold my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for <em>it is</em> good.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen <em>his desire</em> upon mine enemies.
