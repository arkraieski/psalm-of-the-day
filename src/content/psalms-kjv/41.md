<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blessed <em>is</em> he that considereth the poor: the LORD will deliver him in time of trouble.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD will preserve him, and keep him alive; <em>and</em> he shall be blessed upon the earth: and thou wilt not deliver him unto the will of his enemies.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The LORD will strengthen him upon the bed of languishing: thou wilt make all his bed in his sickness.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I said, LORD, be merciful unto me: heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Mine enemies speak evil of me, When shall he die, and his name perish?

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> And if he come to see <em>me</em>, he speaketh vanity: his heart gathereth iniquity to itself; <em>when</em> he goeth abroad, he telleth <em>it</em>.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> All that hate me whisper together against me: against me do they devise my hurt.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> An evil disease, <em>say they</em>, cleaveth fast unto him: and <em>now</em> that he lieth he shall rise up no more.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted, which did eat of my bread, hath lifted up <em>his</em> heel against me.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> But thou, O LORD, be merciful unto me, and raise me up, that I may requite them.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> By this I know that thou favourest me, because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity, and settest me before thy face for ever.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Blessed <em>be</em> the LORD God of Israel from everlasting, and to everlasting. Amen, and Amen.
