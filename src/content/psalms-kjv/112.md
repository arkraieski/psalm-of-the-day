<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise ye the LORD. Blessed <em>is</em> the man <em>that</em> feareth the LORD, <em>that</em> delighteth greatly in his commandments.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> His seed shall be mighty upon earth: the generation of the upright shall be blessed.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Wealth and riches <em>shall be</em> in his house: and his righteousness endureth for ever.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness: <em>he is</em> gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth: he will guide his affairs with discretion.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Surely he shall not be moved for ever: the righteous shall be in everlasting remembrance.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> His heart <em>is</em> established, he shall not be afraid, until he see <em>his desire</em> upon his enemies.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He hath dispersed, he hath given to the poor; his righteousness endureth for ever; his horn shall be exalted with honour.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The wicked shall see <em>it</em>, and be grieved; he shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away: the desire of the wicked shall perish.
