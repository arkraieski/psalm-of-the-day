<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that <em>be</em> above the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them praise the name of the LORD: for he commanded, and they were created.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Praise the LORD from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps:

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Fire, and hail; snow, and vapour; stormy wind fulfilling his word:

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars:

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl:

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth:

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children:

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Let them praise the name of the LORD: for his name alone is excellent; his glory <em>is</em> above the earth and heaven.

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; <em>even</em> of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD.
