<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I cried unto the LORD with my voice; with my voice unto the LORD did I make my supplication.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I poured out my complaint before him; I shewed before him my trouble.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, then thou knewest my path. In the way wherein I walked have they privily laid a snare for me.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I looked on <em>my</em> right hand, and beheld, but <em>there was</em> no man that would know me: refuge failed me; no man cared for my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I cried unto thee, O LORD: I said, Thou <em>art</em> my refuge <em>and</em> my portion in the land of the living.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Attend unto my cry; for I am brought very low: deliver me from my persecutors; for they are stronger than I.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name: the righteous shall compass me about; for thou shalt deal bountifully with me.
