<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blessed <em>is</em> every one that feareth the LORD; that walketh in his ways.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy <em>shalt</em> thou <em>be</em>, and <em>it shall be</em> well with thee.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Thy wife <em>shall be</em> as a fruitful vine by the sides of thine house: thy children like olive plants round about thy table.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The LORD shall bless thee out of Zion: and thou shalt see the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children, <em>and</em> peace upon Israel.
