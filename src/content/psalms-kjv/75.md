<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, <em>unto thee</em> do we give thanks: for <em>that</em> thy name is near thy wondrous works declare.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> When I shall receive the congregation I will judge uprightly.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the horn:

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Lift not up your horn on high: speak <em>not with</em> a stiff neck.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For promotion <em>cometh</em> neither from the east, nor from the west, nor from the south.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> But God <em>is</em> the judge: he putteth down one, and setteth up another.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> For in the hand of the LORD <em>there is</em> a cup, and the wine is red; it is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same: but the dregs thereof, all the wicked of the earth shall wring <em>them</em> out, <em>and</em> drink <em>them</em>.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off; <em>but</em> the horns of the righteous shall be exalted.
