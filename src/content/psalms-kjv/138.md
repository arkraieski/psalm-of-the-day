<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I will praise thee with my whole heart: before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, <em>and</em> strengthenedst me <em>with</em> strength in my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O LORD, when they hear the words of thy mouth.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the LORD: for great <em>is</em> the glory of the LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Though the LORD <em>be</em> high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly: but the proud he knoweth afar off.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD will perfect <em>that which</em> concerneth me: thy mercy, O LORD, <em>endureth</em> for ever: forsake not the works of thine own hands.
