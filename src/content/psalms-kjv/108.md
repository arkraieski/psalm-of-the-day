<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Awake, psaltery and harp: I <em>myself</em> will awake early.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For thy mercy <em>is</em> great above the heavens: and thy truth <em>reacheth</em> unto the clouds.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth;

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> That thy beloved may be delivered: save <em>with</em> thy right hand, and answer me.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Gilead <em>is</em> mine; Manasseh <em>is</em> mine; Ephraim also <em>is</em> the strength of mine head; Judah <em>is</em> my lawgiver;

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Moab <em>is</em> my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <em>Wilt</em> not <em>thou</em>, O God, <em>who</em> hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Give us help from trouble: for vain <em>is</em> the help of man.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Through God we shall do valiantly: for he <em>it is that</em> shall tread down our enemies.
