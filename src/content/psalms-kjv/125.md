<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> They that trust in the LORD <em>shall be</em> as mount Zion, <em>which</em> cannot be removed, <em>but</em> abideth for ever.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> As the mountains <em>are</em> round about Jerusalem, so the LORD <em>is</em> round about his people from henceforth even for ever.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For the rod of the wicked shall not rest upon the lot of the righteous; lest the righteous put forth their hands unto iniquity.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Do good, O LORD, unto <em>those that be</em> good, and <em>to them that are</em> upright in their hearts.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> As for such as turn aside unto their crooked ways, the LORD shall lead them forth with the workers of iniquity: <em>but</em> peace <em>shall be</em> upon Israel.
