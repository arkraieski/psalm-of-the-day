<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee;

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion;

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up <em>our</em> banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Some <em>trust</em> in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the LORD our God.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Save, LORD: let the king hear us when we call.
