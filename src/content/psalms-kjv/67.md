<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God be merciful unto us, and bless us; <em>and</em> cause his face to shine upon us; Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <em>Then</em> shall the earth yield her increase; <em>and</em> God, <em>even</em> our own God, shall bless us.
