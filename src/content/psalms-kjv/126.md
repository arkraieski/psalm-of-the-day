<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> When the LORD turned again the captivity of Zion, we were like them that dream.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Then was our mouth filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing: then said they among the heathen, The LORD hath done great things for them.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The LORD hath done great things for us; <em>whereof</em> we are glad.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Turn again our captivity, O LORD, as the streams in the south.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They that sow in tears shall reap in joy.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves <em>with him</em>.
