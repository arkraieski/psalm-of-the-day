<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God standeth in the congregation of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> How long will ye judge unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked? Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Defend the poor and fatherless: do justice to the afflicted and needy.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Deliver the poor and needy: rid <em>them</em> out of the hand of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in darkness: all the foundations of the earth are out of course.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I have said, Ye <em>are</em> gods; and all of you <em>are</em> children of the most High.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Arise, O God, judge the earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations.
