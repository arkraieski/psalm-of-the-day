<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Lord, hear my voice: let thine ears be attentive to the voice of my supplications.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> If thou, LORD, shouldest mark iniquities, O Lord, who shall stand?

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> But <em>there is</em> forgiveness with thee, that thou mayest be feared.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I wait for the LORD, my soul doth wait, and in his word do I hope.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> My soul <em>waiteth</em> for the Lord more than they that watch for the morning: <em>I say, more than</em> they that watch for the morning.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let Israel hope in the LORD: for with the LORD <em>there is</em> mercy, and with him <em>is</em> plenteous redemption.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> And he shall redeem Israel from all his iniquities.
