<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> LORD, how are they increased that trouble me! many <em>are</em> they that rise up against me.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Many <em>there be</em> which say of my soul, <em>There is</em> no help for him in God. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> But thou, O LORD, <em>art</em> a shield for me; my glory, and the lifter up of mine head.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set <em>themselves</em> against me round about.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies <em>upon</em> the cheek bone; thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Salvation <em>belongeth</em> unto the LORD: thy blessing <em>is</em> upon thy people. Selah.
