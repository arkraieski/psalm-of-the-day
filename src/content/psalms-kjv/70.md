<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <em>Make haste</em>, O God, to deliver me; make haste to help me, O LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, Aha, aha.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> But I <em>am</em> poor and needy: make haste unto me, O God: thou <em>art</em> my help and my deliverer; O LORD, make no tarrying.
