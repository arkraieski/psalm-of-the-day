<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For the LORD most high <em>is</em> terrible; <em>he is</em> a great King over all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> God is gone up with a shout, the LORD with the sound of a trumpet.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our King, sing praises.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> For God <em>is</em> the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> God reigneth over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne of his holiness.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The princes of the people are gathered together, <em>even</em> the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth <em>belong</em> unto God: he is greatly exalted.
