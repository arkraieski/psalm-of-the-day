<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> How long wilt thou forget me, O LORD? for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy face from me?

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> How long shall I take counsel in my soul, <em>having</em> sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Consider <em>and</em> hear me, O LORD my God: lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the <em>sleep of</em> death;

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; <em>and</em> those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> But I have trusted in thy mercy; my heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will sing unto the LORD, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
