<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth, may Israel now say:

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth: yet they have not prevailed against me.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The plowers plowed upon my back: they made long their furrows.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> righteous: he hath cut asunder the cords of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them all be confounded and turned back that hate Zion.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Let them be as the grass <em>upon</em> the housetops, which withereth afore it groweth up:

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Wherewith the mower filleth not his hand; nor he that bindeth sheaves his bosom.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Neither do they which go by say, The blessing of the LORD <em>be</em> upon you: we bless you in the name of the LORD.
