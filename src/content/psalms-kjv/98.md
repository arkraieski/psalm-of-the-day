<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O sing unto the LORD a new song; for he hath done marvellous things: his right hand, and his holy arm, hath gotten him the victory.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD hath made known his salvation: his righteousness hath he openly shewed in the sight of the heathen.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He hath remembered his mercy and his truth toward the house of Israel: all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Sing unto the LORD with the harp; with the harp, and the voice of a psalm.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> With trumpets and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the LORD, the King.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Let the floods clap <em>their</em> hands: let the hills be joyful together

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Before the LORD; for he cometh to judge the earth: with righteousness shall he judge the world, and the people with equity.
