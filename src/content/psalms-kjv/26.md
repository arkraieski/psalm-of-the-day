title: ~
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Judge me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; <em>therefore</em> I shall not slide.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my reins and my heart.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For thy lovingkindness <em>is</em> before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD:

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> LORD, I have loved the habitation of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men:

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> In whose hands <em>is</em> mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the LORD.
