<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all ye lands.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Serve the LORD with gladness: come before his presence with singing.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Know ye that the LORD he <em>is</em> God: <em>it is</em> he <em>that</em> hath made us, and not we ourselves; <em>we are</em> his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, <em>and</em> into his courts with praise: be thankful unto him, <em>and</em> bless his name.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For the LORD <em>is</em> good; his mercy <em>is</em> everlasting; and his truth <em>endureth</em> to all generations.
