testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Do ye indeed speak righteousness, O congregation? do ye judge uprightly, O ye sons of men?

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yea, in heart ye work wickedness; ye weigh the violence of your hands in the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The wicked are estranged from the womb: they go astray as soon as they be born, speaking lies.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Their poison <em>is</em> like the poison of a serpent: <em>they are</em> like the deaf adder <em>that</em> stoppeth her ear;

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Which will not hearken to the voice of charmers, charming never so wisely.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Break their teeth, O God, in their mouth: break out the great teeth of the young lions, O LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let them melt away as waters <em>which</em> run continually: <em>when</em> he bendeth <em>his bow to shoot</em> his arrows, let them be as cut in pieces.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> As a snail <em>which</em> melteth, let <em>every one of them</em> pass away: <em>like</em> the untimely birth of a woman, <em>that</em> they may not see the sun.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Before your pots can feel the thorns, he shall take them away as with a whirlwind, both living, and in <em>his</em> wrath.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The righteous shall rejoice when he seeth the vengeance: he shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> So that a man shall say, Verily <em>there is</em> a reward for the righteous: verily he is a God that judgeth in the earth.
