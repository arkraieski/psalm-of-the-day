<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O LORD our Lord, how excellent <em>is</em> thy name in all the earth! who hast set thy glory above the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies, that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers, the moon and the stars, which thou hast ordained;

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> What is man, that thou art mindful of him? and the son of man, that thou visitest him?

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with glory and honour.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Thou madest him to have dominion over the works of thy hands; thou hast put all <em>things</em> under his feet:

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> All sheep and oxen, yea, and the beasts of the field;

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The fowl of the air, and the fish of the sea, <em>and whatsoever</em> passeth through the paths of the seas.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> O LORD our Lord, how excellent <em>is</em> thy name in all the earth!
