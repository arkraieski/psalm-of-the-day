<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul trusteth in thee: yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I make my refuge, until <em>these</em> calamities be overpast.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I will cry unto God most high; unto God that performeth <em>all things</em> for me.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He shall send from heaven, and save me <em>from</em> the reproach of him that would swallow me up. Selah. God shall send forth his mercy and his truth.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> My soul <em>is</em> among lions: <em>and</em> I lie <em>even among</em> them that are set on fire, <em>even</em> the sons of men, whose teeth <em>are</em> spears and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; <em>let</em> thy glory <em>be</em> above all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> They have prepared a net for my steps; my soul is bowed down: they have digged a pit before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen <em>themselves</em>. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed: I will sing and give praise.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp: I <em>myself</em> will awake early.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people: I will sing unto thee among the nations.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> For thy mercy <em>is</em> great unto the heavens, and thy truth unto the clouds.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: <em>let</em> thy glory <em>be</em> above all the earth.
