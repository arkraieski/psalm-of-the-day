testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O LORD, rebuke me not in thine anger, neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Have mercy upon me, O LORD; for I <em>am</em> weak: O LORD, heal me; for my bones are vexed.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> My soul is also sore vexed: but thou, O LORD, how long?

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Return, O LORD, deliver my soul: oh save me for thy mercies' sake.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For in death <em>there is</em> no remembrance of thee: in the grave who shall give thee thanks?

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I am weary with my groaning; all the night make I my bed to swim; I water my couch with my tears.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Mine eye is consumed because of grief; it waxeth old because of all mine enemies.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; for the LORD hath heard the voice of my weeping.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The LORD hath heard my supplication; the LORD will receive my prayer.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Let all mine enemies be ashamed and sore vexed: let them return <em>and</em> be ashamed suddenly.
