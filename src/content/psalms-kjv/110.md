<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Thy people <em>shall be</em> willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou <em>art</em> a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill <em>the places</em> with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.
