testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> O sing unto the LORD a new song: sing unto the LORD, all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Sing unto the LORD, bless his name; shew forth his salvation from day to day.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Declare his glory among the heathen, his wonders among all people.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For the LORD <em>is</em> great, and greatly to be praised: he <em>is</em> to be feared above all gods.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For all the gods of the nations <em>are</em> idols: but the LORD made the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Honour and majesty <em>are</em> before him: strength and beauty <em>are</em> in his sanctuary.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Give unto the LORD, O ye kindreds of the people, give unto the LORD glory and strength.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Give unto the LORD the glory <em>due unto</em> his name: bring an offering, and come into his courts.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> O worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness: fear before him, all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Say among the heathen <em>that</em> the LORD reigneth: the world also shall be established that it shall not be moved: he shall judge the people righteously.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Let the field be joyful, and all that <em>is</em> therein: then shall all the trees of the wood rejoice

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Before the LORD: for he cometh, for he cometh to judge the earth: he shall judge the world with righteousness, and the people with his truth.
