<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Unto thee lift I up mine eyes, O thou that dwellest in the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Behold, as the eyes of servants <em>look</em> unto the hand of their masters, <em>and</em> as the eyes of a maiden unto the hand of her mistress; so our eyes <em>wait</em> upon the LORD our God, until that he have mercy upon us.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Have mercy upon us, O LORD, have mercy upon us: for we are exceedingly filled with contempt.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scorning of those that are at ease, <em>and</em> with the contempt of the proud.
