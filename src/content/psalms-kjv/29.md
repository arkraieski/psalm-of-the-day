<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the LORD glory and strength.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Give unto the LORD the glory due unto his name; worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The voice of the LORD <em>is</em> upon the waters: the God of glory thundereth: the LORD <em>is</em> upon many waters.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The voice of the LORD <em>is</em> powerful; the voice of the LORD <em>is</em> full of majesty.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The voice of the LORD breaketh the cedars; yea, the LORD breaketh the cedars of Lebanon.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young unicorn.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The voice of the LORD divideth the flames of fire.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The voice of the LORD shaketh the wilderness; the LORD shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The voice of the LORD maketh the hinds to calve, and discovereth the forests: and in his temple doth every one speak of <em>his</em> glory.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The LORD sitteth upon the flood; yea, the LORD sitteth King for ever.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> The LORD will give strength unto his people; the LORD will bless his people with peace.
