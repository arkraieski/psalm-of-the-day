<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Deliver me, O LORD, from the evil man: preserve me from the violent man;

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Which imagine mischiefs in <em>their</em> heart; continually are they gathered together <em>for</em> war.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent; adders' poison <em>is</em> under their lips. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked; preserve me from the violent man; who have purposed to overthrow my goings.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The proud have hid a snare for me, and cords; they have spread a net by the wayside; they have set gins for me. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I said unto the LORD, Thou <em>art</em> my God: hear the voice of my supplications, O LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> O GOD the Lord, the strength of my salvation, thou hast covered my head in the day of battle.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Grant not, O LORD, the desires of the wicked: further not his wicked device; <em>lest</em> they exalt themselves. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <em>As for</em> the head of those that compass me about, let the mischief of their own lips cover them.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Let burning coals fall upon them: let them be cast into the fire; into deep pits, that they rise not up again.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let not an evil speaker be established in the earth: evil shall hunt the violent man to overthrow <em>him</em>.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> I know that the LORD will maintain the cause of the afflicted, <em>and</em> the right of the poor.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Surely the righteous shall give thanks unto thy name: the upright shall dwell in thy presence.
