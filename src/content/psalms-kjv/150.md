<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.
