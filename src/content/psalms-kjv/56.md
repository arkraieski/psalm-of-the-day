<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Mine enemies would daily swallow <em>me</em> up: for <em>they be</em> many that fight against me, O thou most High.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear what flesh can do unto me.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts <em>are</em> against me for evil.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps, when they wait for my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Shall they escape by iniquity? in <em>thine</em> anger cast down the people, O God.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy bottle: <em>are they</em> not in thy book?

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> When I cry <em>unto thee</em>, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for God <em>is</em> for me.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> In God will I praise <em>his</em> word: in the LORD will I praise <em>his</em> word.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Thy vows <em>are</em> upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> For thou hast delivered my soul from death: <em>wilt</em> not <em>thou deliver</em> my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living?
