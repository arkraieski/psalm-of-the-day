<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Great <em>is</em> the LORD, and greatly to be praised in the city of our God, <em>in</em> the mountain of his holiness.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, <em>is</em> mount Zion, <em>on</em> the sides of the north, the city of the great King.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> God is known in her palaces for a refuge.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They saw <em>it, and</em> so they marvelled; they were troubled, <em>and</em> hasted away.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Fear took hold upon them there, <em>and</em> pain, as of a woman in travail.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish with an east wind.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy temple.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> According to thy name, O God, so <em>is</em> thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of righteousness.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let mount Zion rejoice, let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Walk about Zion, and go round about her: tell the towers thereof.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Mark ye well her bulwarks, consider her palaces; that ye may tell <em>it</em> to the generation following.

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> For this God <em>is</em> our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide <em>even</em> unto death.
