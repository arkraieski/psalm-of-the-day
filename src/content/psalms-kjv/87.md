<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> His foundation <em>is</em> in the holy mountains.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD loveth the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this <em>man</em> was born there.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The LORD shall count, when he writeth up the people, <em>that</em> this <em>man</em> was born there. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> As well the singers as the players on instruments <em>shall be there</em>: all my springs <em>are</em> in thee.
