<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The LORD reigneth; let the people tremble: he sitteth <em>between</em> the cherubims; let the earth be moved.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> great in Zion; and he <em>is</em> high above all the people.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them praise thy great and terrible name; <em>for</em> it <em>is</em> holy.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The king's strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Exalt ye the LORD our God, and worship at his footstool; <em>for</em> he <em>is</em> holy.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name; they called upon the LORD, and he answered them.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance <em>that</em> he gave them.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Thou answeredst them, O LORD our God: thou wast a God that forgavest them, though thou tookest vengeance of their inventions.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Exalt the LORD our God, and worship at his holy hill; for the LORD our God <em>is</em> holy.
