testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hear my prayer, O LORD, give ear to my supplications: in thy faithfulness answer me, <em>and</em> in thy righteousness.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> And enter not into judgment with thy servant: for in thy sight shall no man living be justified.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For the enemy hath persecuted my soul; he hath smitten my life down to the ground; he hath made me to dwell in darkness, as those that have been long dead.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Therefore is my spirit overwhelmed within me; my heart within me is desolate.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I remember the days of old; I meditate on all thy works; I muse on the work of thy hands.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I stretch forth my hands unto thee: my soul <em>thirsteth</em> after thee, as a thirsty land. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Hear me speedily, O LORD: my spirit faileth: hide not thy face from me, lest I be like unto them that go down into the pit.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Cause me to hear thy lovingkindness in the morning; for in thee do I trust: cause me to know the way wherein I should walk; for I lift up my soul unto thee.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Deliver me, O LORD, from mine enemies: I flee unto thee to hide me.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Teach me to do thy will; for thou <em>art</em> my God: thy spirit <em>is</em> good; lead me into the land of uprightness.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Quicken me, O LORD, for thy name's sake: for thy righteousness' sake bring my soul out of trouble.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> And of thy mercy cut off mine enemies, and destroy all them that afflict my soul: for I <em>am</em> thy servant.
