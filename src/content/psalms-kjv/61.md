testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my prayer.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the rock <em>that</em> is higher than I.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For thou hast been a shelter for me, <em>and</em> a strong tower from the enemy.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I will abide in thy tabernacle for ever: I will trust in the covert of thy wings. Selah.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For thou, O God, hast heard my vows: thou hast given <em>me</em> the heritage of those that fear thy name.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Thou wilt prolong the king's life: <em>and</em> his years as many generations.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare mercy and truth, <em>which</em> may preserve him.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my vows.
