<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> If <em>it had not been</em> the LORD who was on our side, now may Israel say;

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> If <em>it had not been</em> the LORD who was on our side, when men rose up against us:

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Then they had swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us:

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Then the waters had overwhelmed us, the stream had gone over our soul:

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Then the proud waters had gone over our soul.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Blessed <em>be</em> the LORD, who hath not given us <em>as</em> a prey to their teeth.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Our help <em>is</em> in the name of the LORD, who made heaven and earth.
