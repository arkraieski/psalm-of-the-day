title: ~
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Unto thee will I cry, O LORD my rock; be not silent to me: lest, <em>if</em> thou be silent to me, I become like them that go down into the pit.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Hear the voice of my supplications, when I cry unto thee, when I lift up my hands toward thy holy oracle.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Draw me not away with the wicked, and with the workers of iniquity, which speak peace to their neighbours, but mischief <em>is</em> in their hearts.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Give them according to their deeds, and according to the wickedness of their endeavours: give them after the work of their hands; render to them their desert.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Because they regard not the works of the LORD, nor the operation of his hands, he shall destroy them, and not build them up.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Blessed <em>be</em> the LORD, because he hath heard the voice of my supplications.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> my strength and my shield; my heart trusted in him, and I am helped: therefore my heart greatly rejoiceth; and with my song will I praise him.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> their strength, and he <em>is</em> the saving strength of his anointed.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Save thy people, and bless thine inheritance: feed them also, and lift them up for ever.
