<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise ye the LORD. Praise, O ye servants of the LORD, praise the name of the LORD.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Blessed be the name of the LORD from this time forth and for evermore.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same the LORD'S name <em>is</em> to be praised.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> high above all nations, <em>and</em> his glory above the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Who <em>is</em> like unto the LORD our God, who dwelleth on high,

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Who humbleth <em>himself</em> to behold <em>the things that are</em> in heaven, and in the earth!

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, <em>and</em> lifteth the needy out of the dunghill;

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> That he may set <em>him</em> with princes, <em>even</em> with the princes of his people.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He maketh the barren woman to keep house, <em>and to be</em> a joyful mother of children. Praise ye the LORD.
