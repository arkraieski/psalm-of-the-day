<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, <em>and</em> his praise in the congregation of saints.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <em>Let</em> the high <em>praises</em> of God <em>be</em> in their mouth, and a twoedged sword in their hand;

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> To execute vengeance upon the heathen, <em>and</em> punishments upon the people;

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD.
