<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The LORD reigneth, he is clothed with majesty; the LORD is clothed with strength, <em>wherewith</em> he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished, that it cannot be moved.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Thy throne <em>is</em> established of old: thou <em>art</em> from everlasting.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their waves.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The LORD on high <em>is</em> mightier than the noise of many waters, <em>yea, than</em> the mighty waves of the sea.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Thy testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O LORD, for ever.
