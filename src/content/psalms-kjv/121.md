<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> My help <em>cometh</em> from the LORD, which made heaven and earth.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Behold, he that keepeth Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The LORD <em>is</em> thy keeper: the LORD <em>is</em> thy shade upon thy right hand.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.
