testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation: O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For thou <em>art</em> the God of my strength: why dost thou cast me off? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> O send out thy light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Then will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea, upon the harp will I praise thee, O God my God.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, <em>who is</em> the health of my countenance, and my God.
