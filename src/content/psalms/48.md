---
number: 48
title: "A Song. A Psalm by the sons of Korah."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised,
in the city of our God, in his holy mountain.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Beautiful in elevation, the joy of the whole earth,
is Mount Zion, on the north sides,
the city of the great King.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> God has shown himself in her citadels as a refuge.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For, behold, the kings assembled themselves,
they passed by together.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They saw it, then they were amazed.
They were dismayed.
They hurried away.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Trembling took hold of them there,
pain, as of a woman in travail.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> With the east wind, you break the ships of Tarshish.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> As we have heard, so we have seen,
in the city of Yahweh of Armies, in the city of our God.
God will establish it forever.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> We have thought about your loving kindness, God,
in the middle of your temple.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> As is your name, God,
so is your praise to the ends of the earth.
Your right hand is full of righteousness.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let Mount Zion be glad!
Let the daughters of Judah rejoice because of your judgments.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Walk about Zion, and go around her.
Number its towers.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Notice her bulwarks.
Consider her palaces,
that you may tell it to the next generation.

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> For this God is our God forever and ever.
He will be our guide even to death.
