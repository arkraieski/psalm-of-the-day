<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
Praise, you servants of Yahweh,
praise Yahweh’s name.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Blessed be Yahweh’s name,
from this time forward and forever more.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> From the rising of the sun to its going down,
Yahweh’s name is to be praised.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahweh is high above all nations,
his glory above the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Who is like Yahweh, our God,
who has his seat on high,

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Who stoops down to see in heaven and in the earth?

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He raises up the poor out of the dust.
Lifts up the needy from the ash heap,

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> that he may set him with princes,
even with the princes of his people.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He settles the barren woman in her home
as a joyful mother of children.
Praise Yah!
