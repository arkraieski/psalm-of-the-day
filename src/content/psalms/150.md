<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
Praise God in his sanctuary!
Praise him in his heavens for his acts of power!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise him for his mighty acts!
Praise him according to his excellent greatness!

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise him with the sounding of the trumpet!
Praise him with harp and lyre!

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him with tambourine and dancing!
Praise him with stringed instruments and flute!

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Praise him with loud cymbals!
Praise him with resounding cymbals!

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Let everything that has breath praise Yah!
Praise Yah!
