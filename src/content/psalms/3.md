---
number: 3
title: "A Psalm by David, when he fled from Absalom his son."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh, how my adversaries have increased!
Many are those who rise up against me.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Many there are who say of my soul,
“There is no help for him in God.”

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> But you, Yahweh, are a shield around me,
my glory, and the one who lifts up my head.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I cry to Yahweh with my voice,
and he answers me out of his holy hill.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I laid myself down and slept.
I awakened; for Yahweh sustains me.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will not be afraid of tens of thousands of people
who have set themselves against me on every side.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Arise, Yahweh!
Save me, my God!
For you have struck all of my enemies on the cheek bone.
You have broken the teeth of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Salvation belongs to Yahweh.
May your blessing be on your people.
