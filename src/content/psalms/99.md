testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh reigns! Let the peoples tremble.
He sits enthroned among the cherubim.
Let the earth be moved.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh is great in Zion.
He is high above all the peoples.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them praise your great and awesome name.
He is Holy!


<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The King’s strength also loves justice.
You establish equity.
You execute justice and righteousness in Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Exalt Yahweh our God.
Worship at his footstool.
He is Holy!


<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Moses and Aaron were among his priests,
Samuel was among those who call on his name.
They called on Yahweh, and he answered them.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He spoke to them in the pillar of cloud.
They kept his testimonies,
the statute that he gave them.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You answered them, Yahweh our God.
You are a God who forgave them,
although you took vengeance for their doings.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Exalt Yahweh, our God.
Worship at his holy hill,
for Yahweh, our God, is holy!
