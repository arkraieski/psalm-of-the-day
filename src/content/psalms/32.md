---
number: 32
title: "By David. A contemplative psalm."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blessed is he whose disobedience is forgiven,
whose sin is covered.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Blessed is the man to whom Yahweh doesn’t impute iniquity,
in whose spirit there is no deceit.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> When I kept silence, my bones wasted away through my groaning all day long.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For day and night your hand was heavy on me.
My strength was sapped in the heat of summer.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I acknowledged my sin to you.
I didn’t hide my iniquity.
I said, I will confess my transgressions to Yahweh,
and you forgave the iniquity of my sin.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For this, let everyone who is godly pray to you in a time when you may be found.
Surely when the great waters overflow, they shall not reach to him.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> You are my hiding place.
You will preserve me from trouble.
You will surround me with songs of deliverance.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I will instruct you and teach you in the way which you shall go.
I will counsel you with my eye on you.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Don’t be like the horse, or like the mule, which have no understanding,
who are controlled by bit and bridle, or else they will not come near to you.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Many sorrows come to the wicked,
but loving kindness shall surround him who trusts in Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Be glad in Yahweh, and rejoice, you righteous!
Shout for joy, all you who are upright in heart!
