---
number: 47
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by the sons of Korah."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Oh clap your hands, all you nations.
Shout to God with the voice of triumph!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For Yahweh Most High is awesome.
He is a great King over all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He subdues nations under us,
and peoples under our feet.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He chooses our inheritance for us,
the glory of Jacob whom he loved.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> God has gone up with a shout,
Yahweh with the sound of a trumpet.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Sing praises to God! Sing praises!
Sing praises to our King! Sing praises!

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> For God is the King of all the earth.
Sing praises with understanding.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> God reigns over the nations.
God sits on his holy throne.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The princes of the peoples are gathered together,
the people of the God of Abraham.
For the shields of the earth belong to God.
He is greatly exalted!
