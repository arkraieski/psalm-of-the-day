<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh reigns!
He is clothed with majesty!
Yahweh is armed with strength.
The world also is established.
It can’t be moved.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Your throne is established from long ago.
You are from everlasting.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The floods have lifted up, Yahweh,
the floods have lifted up their voice.
The floods lift up their waves.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Above the voices of many waters,
the mighty breakers of the sea,
Yahweh on high is mighty.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Your statutes stand firm.
Holiness adorns your house,
Yahweh, forever more.
