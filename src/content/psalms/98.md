---
number: 98
title: "A Psalm."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sing to Yahweh a new song,
for he has done marvelous things!
His right hand and his holy arm have worked salvation for him.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh has made known his salvation.
He has openly shown his righteousness in the sight of the nations.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He has remembered his loving kindness and his faithfulness toward the house of Israel.
All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Make a joyful noise to Yahweh, all the earth!
Burst out and sing for joy, yes, sing praises!

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Sing praises to Yahweh with the harp,
with the harp and the voice of melody.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> With trumpets and sound of the ram’s horn,
make a joyful noise before the King, Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let the sea roar with its fullness;
the world, and those who dwell therein.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Let the rivers clap their hands.
Let the mountains sing for joy together.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Let them sing before Yahweh,
for he comes to judge the earth.
He will judge the world with righteousness,
and the peoples with equity.
