---
number: 124
title: "A Song of Ascents. By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> If it had not been Yahweh who was on our side,
let Israel now say,

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> if it had not been Yahweh who was on our side,
when men rose up against us;

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> then they would have swallowed us up alive,
when their wrath was kindled against us;

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> then the waters would have overwhelmed us,
the stream would have gone over our soul;

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> then the proud waters would have gone over our soul.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Blessed be Yahweh,
who has not given us as a prey to their teeth.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Our soul has escaped like a bird out of the fowler’s snare.
The snare is broken, and we have escaped.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Our help is in Yahweh’s name,
who made heaven and earth.
