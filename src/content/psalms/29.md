---
number: 29
title: "A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Ascribe to Yahweh, you sons of the mighty,
ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name.
Worship Yahweh in holy array.


<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Yahweh’s voice is on the waters.
The God of glory thunders, even Yahweh on many waters.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahweh’s voice is powerful.
Yahweh’s voice is full of majesty.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Yahweh’s voice breaks the cedars.
Yes, Yahweh breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He makes them also to skip like a calf;
Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Yahweh’s voice strikes with flashes of lightning.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh’s voice shakes the wilderness.
Yahweh shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Yahweh’s voice makes the deer calve,
and strips the forests bare.
In his temple everything says, “Glory!”


<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Yahweh sat enthroned at the Flood.
Yes, Yahweh sits as King forever.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Yahweh will give strength to his people.
Yahweh will bless his people with peace.
