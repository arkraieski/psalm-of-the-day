---
number: 30
title: "A Psalm. A Song for the Dedication of the Temple. By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I will extol you, Yahweh, for you have raised me up,
and have not made my foes to rejoice over me.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh my God, I cried to you,
and you have healed me.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Yahweh, you have brought up my soul from Sheol.
You have kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Sing praise to Yahweh, you saints of his.
Give thanks to his holy name.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For his anger is but for a moment.
His favor is for a lifetime.
Weeping may stay for the night,
but joy comes in the morning.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> As for me, I said in my prosperity,
“I shall never be moved.”

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> You, Yahweh, when you favored me, made my mountain stand strong;
but when you hid your face, I was troubled.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I cried to you, Yahweh.
I made supplication to the Lord:

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> “What profit is there in my destruction, if I go down to the pit?
Shall the dust praise you?
Shall it declare your truth?

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Hear, Yahweh, and have mercy on me.
Yahweh, be my helper.”

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> You have turned my mourning into dancing for me.
You have removed my sackcloth, and clothed me with gladness,

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> to the end that my heart may sing praise to you, and not be silent.
Yahweh my God, I will give thanks to you forever!
