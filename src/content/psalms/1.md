<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blessed is the man who doesn’t walk in the counsel of the wicked,
nor stand on the path of sinners,
nor sit in the seat of scoffers;

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> but his delight is in Yahweh’s law.
On his law he meditates day and night.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He will be like a tree planted by the streams of water,
that produces its fruit in its season,
whose leaf also does not wither.
Whatever he does shall prosper.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The wicked are not so,
but are like the chaff which the wind drives away.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Therefore the wicked shall not stand in the judgment,
nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For Yahweh knows the way of the righteous,
but the way of the wicked shall perish.
