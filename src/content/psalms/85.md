---
number: 85
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by the sons of Korah."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh, you have been favorable to your land.
You have restored the fortunes of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> You have forgiven the iniquity of your people.
You have covered all their sin.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You have taken away all your wrath.
You have turned from the fierceness of your anger.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Turn us, God of our salvation,
and cause your indignation toward us to cease.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Will you be angry with us forever?
Will you draw out your anger to all generations?

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Won’t you revive us again,
that your people may rejoice in you?

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Show us your loving kindness, Yahweh.
Grant us your salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I will hear what God, Yahweh, will speak,
for he will speak peace to his people, his saints;
but let them not turn again to folly.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Surely his salvation is near those who fear him,
that glory may dwell in our land.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Mercy and truth meet together.
Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Truth springs out of the earth.
Righteousness has looked down from heaven.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Yes, Yahweh will give that which is good.
Our land will yield its increase.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Righteousness goes before him,
And prepares the way for his steps.
