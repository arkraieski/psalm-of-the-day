---
number: 26
title: "By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity.
I have trusted also in Yahweh without wavering.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me.
Try my heart and my mind.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For your loving kindness is before my eyes.
I have walked in your truth.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I have not sat with deceitful men,
neither will I go in with hypocrites.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I hate the assembly of evildoers,
and will not sit with the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will wash my hands in innocence,
so I will go about your altar, Yahweh,

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> that I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard
and tell of all your wondrous deeds.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house,
the place where your glory dwells.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Don’t gather my soul with sinners,
nor my life with bloodthirsty men;

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> in whose hands is wickedness,
their right hand is full of bribes.


<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> But as for me, I will walk in my integrity.
Redeem me, and be merciful to me.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> My foot stands in an even place.
In the congregations I will bless Yahweh.
