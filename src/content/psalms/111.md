<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart,
in the council of the upright, and in the congregation.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh’s works are great,
pondered by all those who delight in them.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> His work is honor and majesty.
His righteousness endures forever.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He has caused his wonderful works to be remembered.
Yahweh is gracious and merciful.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He has given food to those who fear him.
He always remembers his covenant.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He has shown his people the power of his works,
in giving them the heritage of the nations.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The works of his hands are truth and justice.
All his precepts are sure.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They are established forever and ever.
They are done in truth and uprightness.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He has sent redemption to his people.
He has ordained his covenant forever.
His name is holy and awesome!

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom.
All those who do his work have a good understanding.
His praise endures forever!
