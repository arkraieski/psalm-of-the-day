---
number: 75
title: "For the Chief Musician. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm by Asaph. A song."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> We give thanks to you, God.
We give thanks, for your Name is near.
Men tell about your wondrous works.


<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> When I choose the appointed time,
I will judge blamelessly.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The earth and all its inhabitants quake.
I firmly hold its pillars.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I said to the arrogant, “Don’t boast!”
I said to the wicked, “Don’t lift up the horn.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Don’t lift up your horn on high.
Don’t speak with a stiff neck.”

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For neither from the east, nor from the west,
nor yet from the south, comes exaltation.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> But God is the judge.
He puts down one, and lifts up another.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> For in Yahweh’s hand there is a cup,
full of foaming wine mixed with spices.
He pours it out.
Indeed the wicked of the earth drink and drink it to its very dregs.


<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> But I will declare this forever:
I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> I will cut off all the horns of the wicked,
but the horns of the righteous shall be lifted up.
