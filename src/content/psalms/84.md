---
number: 84
title: "For the Chief Musician. On an instrument of Gath. A Psalm by the sons of Korah."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> How lovely are your dwellings,
Yahweh of Armies!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> My soul longs, and even faints for the courts of Yahweh.
My heart and my flesh cry out for the living God.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Yes, the sparrow has found a home,
and the swallow a nest for herself, where she may have her young,
near your altars, Yahweh of Armies, my King, and my God.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Blessed are those who dwell in your house.
They are always praising you.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Blessed are those whose strength is in you,
who have set their hearts on a pilgrimage.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Passing through the valley of Weeping, they make it a place of springs.
Yes, the autumn rain covers it with blessings.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> They go from strength to strength.
Every one of them appears before God in Zion.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh, God of Armies, hear my prayer.
Listen, God of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Behold, God our shield,
look at the face of your anointed.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> For a day in your courts is better than a thousand.
I would rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God,
than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> For Yahweh God is a sun and a shield.
Yahweh will give grace and glory.
He withholds no good thing from those who walk blamelessly.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Yahweh of Armies,
blessed is the man who trusts in you.
