---
number: 121
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I will lift up my eyes to the hills.
Where does my help come from?

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> My help comes from Yahweh,
who made heaven and earth.


<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He will not allow your foot to be moved.
He who keeps you will not slumber.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Behold, he who keeps Israel
will neither slumber nor sleep.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Yahweh is your keeper.
Yahweh is your shade on your right hand.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The sun will not harm you by day,
nor the moon by night.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Yahweh will keep you from all evil.
He will keep your soul.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh will keep your going out and your coming in,
from this time forward, and forever more.
