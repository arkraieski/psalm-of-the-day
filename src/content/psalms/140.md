---
number: 140
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Deliver me, Yahweh, from the evil man.
Preserve me from the violent man:

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> those who devise mischief in their hearts.
They continually gather themselves together for war.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent.
Viper’s poison is under their lips.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahweh, keep me from the hands of the wicked.
Preserve me from the violent men who have determined to trip my feet.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The proud have hidden a snare for me,
they have spread the cords of a net by the path.
They have set traps for me.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I said to Yahweh, “You are my God.”
Listen to the cry of my petitions, Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Yahweh, the Lord, the strength of my salvation,
you have covered my head in the day of battle.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh, don’t grant the desires of the wicked.
Don’t let their evil plans succeed, or they will become proud.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> As for the head of those who surround me,
let the mischief of their own lips cover them.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Let burning coals fall on them.
Let them be thrown into the fire,
into miry pits, from where they never rise.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> An evil speaker won’t be established in the earth.
Evil will hunt the violent man to overthrow him.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> I know that Yahweh will maintain the cause of the afflicted,
and justice for the needy.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Surely the righteous will give thanks to your name.
The upright will dwell in your presence.
