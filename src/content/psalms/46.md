---
number: 46
title: "For the Chief Musician. By the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. * Alamoth is a musical term."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God is our refuge and strength,
a very present help in trouble.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Therefore we won’t be afraid, though the earth changes,
though the mountains are shaken into the heart of the seas;

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> though its waters roar and are troubled,
though the mountains tremble with their swelling.


<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> There is a river, the streams of which make the city of God glad,
the holy place of the tents of the Most High.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> God is within her. She shall not be moved.
God will help her at dawn.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The nations raged. The kingdoms were moved.
He lifted his voice and the earth melted.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Yahweh of Armies is with us.
The God of Jacob is our refuge.


<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Come, see Yahweh’s works,
what desolations he has made in the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He makes wars cease to the end of the earth.
He breaks the bow, and shatters the spear.
He burns the chariots in the fire.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> “Be still, and know that I am God.
I will be exalted among the nations.
I will be exalted in the earth.”

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Yahweh of Armies is with us.
The God of Jacob is our refuge.
