---
number: 70
title: "For the Chief Musician. By David. A reminder."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hurry, God, to deliver me.
Come quickly to help me, Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let them be disappointed and confounded who seek my soul.
Let those who desire my ruin be turned back in disgrace.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them be turned because of their shame
who say, “Aha! Aha!”

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Let all those who seek you rejoice and be glad in you.
Let those who love your salvation continually say,
“Let God be exalted!”

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> But I am poor and needy.
Come to me quickly, God.
You are my help and my deliverer.
Yahweh, don’t delay.
