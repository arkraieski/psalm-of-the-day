testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sing to Yahweh a new song!
Sing to Yahweh, all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Sing to Yahweh!
Bless his name!
Proclaim his salvation from day to day!

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Declare his glory among the nations,
his marvelous works among all the peoples.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For Yahweh is great, and greatly to be praised!
He is to be feared above all gods.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For all the gods of the peoples are idols,
but Yahweh made the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Honor and majesty are before him.
Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Ascribe to Yahweh, you families of nations,
ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name.
Bring an offering, and come into his courts.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Worship Yahweh in holy array.
Tremble before him, all the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Say among the nations, “Yahweh reigns.”
The world is also established.
It can’t be moved.
He will judge the peoples with equity.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice.
Let the sea roar, and its fullness!

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Let the field and all that is in it exult!
Then all the trees of the woods shall sing for joy

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> before Yahweh; for he comes,
for he comes to judge the earth.
He will judge the world with righteousness,
the peoples with his truth.
