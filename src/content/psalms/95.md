<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Oh come, let’s sing to Yahweh.
Let’s shout aloud to the rock of our salvation!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let’s come before his presence with thanksgiving.
Let’s extol him with songs!

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For Yahweh is a great God,
a great King above all gods.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> In his hand are the deep places of the earth.
The heights of the mountains are also his.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The sea is his, and he made it.
His hands formed the dry land.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Oh come, let’s worship and bow down.
Let’s kneel before Yahweh, our Maker,

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> for he is our God.
We are the people of his pasture,
and the sheep in his care.
Today, oh that you would hear his voice!

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Don’t harden your heart, as at Meribah,
as in the day of Massah in the wilderness,

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> when your fathers tempted me,
tested me, and saw my work.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Forty long years I was grieved with that generation,
and said, “It is a people that errs in their heart.
They have not known my ways.”

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Therefore I swore in my wrath,
“They won’t enter into my rest.”
