<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yahweh!
Sing to Yahweh a new song,
his praise in the assembly of the saints.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let Israel rejoice in him who made them.
Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them praise his name in the dance!
Let them sing praises to him with tambourine and harp!

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For Yahweh takes pleasure in his people.
He crowns the humble with salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let the saints rejoice in honor.
Let them sing for joy on their beds.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> May the high praises of God be in their mouths,
and a two-edged sword in their hand,

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> to execute vengeance on the nations,
and punishments on the peoples;

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> to bind their kings with chains,
and their nobles with fetters of iron;

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> to execute on them the written judgment.
All his saints have this honor.
Praise Yah!
