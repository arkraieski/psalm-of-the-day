---
number: 13
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> How long, Yahweh?
Will you forget me forever?
How long will you hide your face from me?

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> How long shall I take counsel in my soul,
having sorrow in my heart every day?
How long shall my enemy triumph over me?

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Behold, and answer me, Yahweh, my God.
Give light to my eyes, lest I sleep in death;

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> lest my enemy say, “I have prevailed against him;”
lest my adversaries rejoice when I fall.


<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> But I trust in your loving kindness.
My heart rejoices in your salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I will sing to Yahweh,
because he has been good to me.
