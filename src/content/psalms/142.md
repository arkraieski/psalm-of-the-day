---
number: 142
title: "A contemplation by David, when he was in the cave. A Prayer."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I cry with my voice to Yahweh.
With my voice, I ask Yahweh for mercy.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I pour out my complaint before him.
I tell him my troubles.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> When my spirit was overwhelmed within me,
you knew my route.
On the path in which I walk,
they have hidden a snare for me.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Look on my right, and see;
for there is no one who is concerned for me.
Refuge has fled from me.
No one cares for my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I cried to you, Yahweh.
I said, “You are my refuge,
my portion in the land of the living.”

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Listen to my cry,
for I am in desperate need.
Deliver me from my persecutors,
for they are too strong for me.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Bring my soul out of prison,
that I may give thanks to your name.
The righteous will surround me,
for you will be good to me.
