---
number: 110
title: "A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh says to my Lord, “Sit at my right hand,
until I make your enemies your footstool for your feet.”

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh will send out the rod of your strength out of Zion.
Rule among your enemies.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Your people offer themselves willingly in the day of your power, in holy array.
Out of the womb of the morning, you have the dew of your youth.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahweh has sworn, and will not change his mind:
“You are a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek.”

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The Lord is at your right hand.
He will crush kings in the day of his wrath.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He will judge among the nations.
He will heap up dead bodies.
He will crush the ruler of the whole earth.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He will drink of the brook on the way;
therefore he will lift up his head.
