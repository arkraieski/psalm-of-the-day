---
number: 134
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Look! Praise Yahweh, all you servants of Yahweh,
who stand by night in Yahweh’s house!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Lift up your hands in the sanctuary.
Praise Yahweh!

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> May Yahweh bless you from Zion,
even he who made heaven and earth.
