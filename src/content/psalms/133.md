---
number: 133
title: "A Song of Ascents. By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> See how good and how pleasant it is
for brothers to live together in unity!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> It is like the precious oil on the head,
that ran down on the beard,
even Aaron’s beard,
that came down on the edge of his robes,

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> like the dew of Hermon,
that comes down on the hills of Zion;
for there Yahweh gives the blessing,
even life forever more.
