<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good;
for his loving kindness endures forever.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Give thanks to the God of gods;
for his loving kindness endures forever.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Give thanks to the Lord of lords;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> to him who alone does great wonders;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> to him who by understanding made the heavens;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> to him who spread out the earth above the waters;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> to him who made the great lights;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> the sun to rule by day;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> the moon and stars to rule by night;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> to him who struck down the Egyptian firstborn;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> and brought out Israel from among them;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> with a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> to him who divided the Red Sea apart;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> and made Israel to pass through the middle of it;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">15</sup> but overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">16</sup> to him who led his people through the wilderness;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">17</sup> to him who struck great kings;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">18</sup> and killed mighty kings;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Sihon king of the Amorites;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Og king of Bashan;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">21</sup> and gave their land as an inheritance;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">22</sup> even a heritage to Israel his servant;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">23</sup> who remembered us in our low estate;
for his loving kindness endures forever;

<sup class="verse-num">24</sup> and has delivered us from our adversaries;
for his loving kindness endures forever:

<sup class="verse-num">25</sup> who gives food to every creature;
for his loving kindness endures forever.

<sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Oh give thanks to the God of heaven;
for his loving kindness endures forever.
