testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Vindicate me, God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation.
Oh, deliver me from deceitful and wicked men.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For you are the God of my strength. Why have you rejected me?
Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Oh, send out your light and your truth.
Let them lead me.
Let them bring me to your holy hill,
to your tents.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Then I will go to the altar of God,
to God, my exceeding joy.
I will praise you on the harp, God, my God.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Why are you in despair, my soul?
Why are you disturbed within me?
Hope in God!
For I shall still praise him:
my Savior, my helper, and my God.
