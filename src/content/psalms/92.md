---
number: 92
title: "A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> It is a good thing to give thanks to Yahweh,
to sing praises to your name, Most High,

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> to proclaim your loving kindness in the morning,
and your faithfulness every night,

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> with the ten-stringed lute, with the harp,
and with the melody of the lyre.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For you, Yahweh, have made me glad through your work.
I will triumph in the works of your hands.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> How great are your works, Yahweh!
Your thoughts are very deep.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> A senseless man doesn’t know,
neither does a fool understand this:

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> though the wicked spring up as the grass,
and all the evildoers flourish,
they will be destroyed forever.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> But you, Yahweh, are on high forever more.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> For, behold, your enemies, Yahweh,
for, behold, your enemies shall perish.
All the evildoers will be scattered.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> But you have exalted my horn like that of the wild ox.
I am anointed with fresh oil.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> My eye has also seen my enemies.
My ears have heard of the wicked enemies who rise up against me.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree.
He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> They are planted in Yahweh’s house.
They will flourish in our God’s courts.

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> They will still produce fruit in old age.
They will be full of sap and green,

<sup class="verse-num">15</sup> to show that Yahweh is upright.
He is my rock,
and there is no unrighteousness in him.
