---
number: 122
title: "A Song of Ascents. By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I was glad when they said to me,
“Let’s go to Yahweh’s house!”

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Our feet are standing within your gates, Jerusalem,

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Jerusalem, that is built as a city that is compact together,

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> where the tribes go up, even Yah’s tribes,
according to an ordinance for Israel,
to give thanks to Yahweh’s name.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For there are set thrones for judgment,
the thrones of David’s house.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Pray for the peace of Jerusalem.
Those who love you will prosper.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Peace be within your walls,
and prosperity within your palaces.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> For my brothers’ and companions’ sakes,
I will now say, “Peace be within you.”

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> For the sake of the house of Yahweh our God,
I will seek your good.
