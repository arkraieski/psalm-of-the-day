---
number: 23
title: "A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh is my shepherd:
I shall lack nothing.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He makes me lie down in green pastures.
He leads me beside still waters.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He restores my soul.
He guides me in the paths of righteousness for his name’s sake.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,
I will fear no evil, for you are with me.
Your rod and your staff,
they comfort me.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You prepare a table before me
in the presence of my enemies.
You anoint my head with oil.
My cup runs over.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Surely goodness and loving kindness shall follow me all the days of my life,
and I will dwell in Yahweh’s house forever.
