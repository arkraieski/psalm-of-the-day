testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh reigns!
Let the earth rejoice!
Let the multitude of islands be glad!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Clouds and darkness are around him.
Righteousness and justice are the foundation of his throne.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> A fire goes before him,
and burns up his adversaries on every side.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> His lightning lights up the world.
The earth sees, and trembles.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The mountains melt like wax at the presence of Yahweh,
at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The heavens declare his righteousness.
All the peoples have seen his glory.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let all them be shamed who serve engraved images,
who boast in their idols.
Worship him, all you gods!

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Zion heard and was glad.
The daughters of Judah rejoiced
because of your judgments, Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> For you, Yahweh, are most high above all the earth.
You are exalted far above all gods.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You who love Yahweh, hate evil!
He preserves the souls of his saints.
He delivers them out of the hand of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Light is sown for the righteous,
and gladness for the upright in heart.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Be glad in Yahweh, you righteous people!
Give thanks to his holy Name.
