---
number: 67
title: "For the Chief Musician. With stringed instruments. A Psalm. A song."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> May God be merciful to us, bless us,
and cause his face to shine on us.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> That your way may be known on earth,
and your salvation among all nations,

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> let the peoples praise you, God.
Let all the peoples praise you.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Oh let the nations be glad and sing for joy,
for you will judge the peoples with equity,
and govern the nations on earth.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let the peoples praise you, God.
Let all the peoples praise you.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The earth has yielded its increase.
God, even our own God, will bless us.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> God will bless us.
All the ends of the earth shall fear him.
