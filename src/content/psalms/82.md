---
number: 82
title: "A Psalm by Asaph."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God presides in the great assembly.
He judges among the gods.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> “How long will you judge unjustly,
and show partiality to the wicked?”


<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> “Defend the weak, the poor, and the fatherless.
Maintain the rights of the poor and oppressed.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Rescue the weak and needy.
Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked.”

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They don’t know, neither do they understand.
They walk back and forth in darkness.
All the foundations of the earth are shaken.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I said, “You are gods,
all of you are sons of the Most High.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Nevertheless you shall die like men,
and fall like one of the rulers.”

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Arise, God, judge the earth,
for you inherit all of the nations.
