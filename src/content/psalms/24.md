---
number: 24
title: "A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The earth is Yahweh’s, with its fullness;
the world, and those who dwell in it.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For he has founded it on the seas,
and established it on the floods.


<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Who may ascend to Yahweh’s hill?
Who may stand in his holy place?

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He who has clean hands and a pure heart;
who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood,
and has not sworn deceitfully.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He shall receive a blessing from Yahweh,
righteousness from the God of his salvation.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> This is the generation of those who seek Him,
who seek your face—even Jacob.


<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Lift up your heads, you gates!
Be lifted up, you everlasting doors,
and the King of glory will come in.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Who is the King of glory?
Yahweh strong and mighty,
Yahweh mighty in battle.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Lift up your heads, you gates;
yes, lift them up, you everlasting doors,
and the King of glory will come in.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Who is this King of glory?
Yahweh of Armies is the King of glory!
