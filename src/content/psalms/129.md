---
number: 129
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Many times they have afflicted me from my youth up.
Let Israel now say,

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> many times they have afflicted me from my youth up,
yet they have not prevailed against me.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The plowers plowed on my back.
They made their furrows long.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahweh is righteous.
He has cut apart the cords of the wicked.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them be disappointed and turned backward,
all those who hate Zion.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Let them be as the grass on the housetops,
which withers before it grows up,

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> with which the reaper doesn’t fill his hand,
nor he who binds sheaves, his bosom.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Neither do those who go by say,
“The blessing of Yahweh be on you.
We bless you in Yahweh’s name.”
