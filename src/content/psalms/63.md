---
number: 63
title: "A Psalm by David, when he was in the desert of Judah."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God, you are my God.
I will earnestly seek you.
My soul thirsts for you.
My flesh longs for you,
in a dry and weary land, where there is no water.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> So I have seen you in the sanctuary,
watching your power and your glory.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Because your loving kindness is better than life,
my lips shall praise you.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> So I will bless you while I live.
I will lift up my hands in your name.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> My soul shall be satisfied as with the richest food.
My mouth shall praise you with joyful lips,

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> when I remember you on my bed,
and think about you in the night watches.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> For you have been my help.
I will rejoice in the shadow of your wings.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> My soul stays close to you.
Your right hand holds me up.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> But those who seek my soul to destroy it
shall go into the lower parts of the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> They shall be given over to the power of the sword.
They shall be jackal food.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> But the king shall rejoice in God.
Everyone who swears by him will praise him,
for the mouth of those who speak lies shall be silenced.
