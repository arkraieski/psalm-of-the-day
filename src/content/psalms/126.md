---
number: 126
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> When Yahweh brought back those who returned to Zion,
we were like those who dream.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Then our mouth was filled with laughter,
and our tongue with singing.
Then they said among the nations,
“Yahweh has done great things for them.”

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Yahweh has done great things for us,
and we are glad.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Restore our fortunes again, Yahweh,
like the streams in the Negev.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Those who sow in tears will reap in joy.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He who goes out weeping, carrying seed for sowing,
will certainly come again with joy, carrying his sheaves.
