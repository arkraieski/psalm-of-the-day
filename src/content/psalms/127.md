---
number: 127
title: "A Song of Ascents. By Solomon."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Unless Yahweh builds the house,
they who build it labor in vain.
Unless Yahweh watches over the city,
the watchman guards it in vain.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> It is vain for you to rise up early,
to stay up late,
eating the bread of toil,
for he gives sleep to his loved ones.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Behold, children are a heritage of Yahweh.
The fruit of the womb is his reward.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> As arrows in the hand of a mighty man,
so are the children of youth.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Happy is the man who has his quiver full of them.
They won’t be disappointed when they speak with their enemies in the gate.
