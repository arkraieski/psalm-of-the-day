---
number: 15
title: "A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary?
Who shall live on your holy hill?

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He who walks blamelessly and does what is right,
and speaks truth in his heart;

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> he who doesn’t slander with his tongue,
nor does evil to his friend,
nor casts slurs against his fellow man;

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> in whose eyes a vile man is despised,
but who honors those who fear Yahweh;
he who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn’t change;

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> he who doesn’t lend out his money for usury,
nor take a bribe against the innocent.
He who does these things shall never be shaken.
