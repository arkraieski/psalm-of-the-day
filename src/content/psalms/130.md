---
number: 130
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Out of the depths I have cried to you, Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Lord, hear my voice.
Let your ears be attentive to the voice of my petitions.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> If you, Yah, kept a record of sins,
Lord, who could stand?

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> But there is forgiveness with you,
therefore you are feared.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I wait for Yahweh.
My soul waits.
I hope in his word.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> My soul longs for the Lord more than watchmen long for the morning,
more than watchmen for the morning.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Israel, hope in Yahweh,
for there is loving kindness with Yahweh.
Abundant redemption is with him.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> He will redeem Israel from all their sins.
