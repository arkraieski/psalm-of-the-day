---
number: 120
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> In my distress, I cried to Yahweh.
He answered me.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Deliver my soul, Yahweh, from lying lips,
from a deceitful tongue.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> What will be given to you, and what will be done more to you,
you deceitful tongue?

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Sharp arrows of the mighty,
with coals of juniper.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Woe is me, that I live in Meshech,
that I dwell among the tents of Kedar!

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> My soul has had her dwelling too long
with him who hates peace.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> I am for peace,
but when I speak, they are for war.
