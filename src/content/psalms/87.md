---
number: 87
title: "A Psalm by the sons of Korah; a Song."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> His foundation is in the holy mountains.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahweh loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Glorious things are spoken about you, city of God.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I will record Rahab and Babylon among those who acknowledge me.
Behold, Philistia, Tyre, and also Ethiopia:
“This one was born there.”

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Yes, of Zion it will be said, “This one and that one was born in her;”
the Most High himself will establish her.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Yahweh will count, when he writes up the peoples,
“This one was born there.”

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Those who sing as well as those who dance say,
“All my springs are in you.”
