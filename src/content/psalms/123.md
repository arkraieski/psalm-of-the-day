---
number: 123
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I lift up my eyes to you,
you who sit in the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Behold, as the eyes of servants look to the hand of their master,
as the eyes of a maid to the hand of her mistress;
so our eyes look to Yahweh, our God,
until he has mercy on us.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Have mercy on us, Yahweh, have mercy on us,
for we have endured much contempt.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scoffing of those who are at ease,
with the contempt of the proud.
