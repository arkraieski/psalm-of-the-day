testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
Blessed is the man who fears Yahweh,
who delights greatly in his commandments.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> His offspring will be mighty in the land.
The generation of the upright will be blessed.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Wealth and riches are in his house.
His righteousness endures forever.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Light dawns in the darkness for the upright,
gracious, merciful, and righteous.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> It is well with the man who deals graciously and lends.
He will maintain his cause in judgment.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For he will never be shaken.
The righteous will be remembered forever.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He will not be afraid of evil news.
His heart is steadfast, trusting in Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> His heart is established.
He will not be afraid in the end when he sees his adversaries.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He has dispersed, he has given to the poor.
His righteousness endures forever.
His horn will be exalted with honor.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The wicked will see it, and be grieved.
He shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away.
The desire of the wicked will perish.
