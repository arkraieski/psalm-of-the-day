testament: "OT"

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
Praise Yahweh from the heavens!
Praise him in the heights!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise him, all his angels!
Praise him, all his army!

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise him, sun and moon!
Praise him, all you shining stars!

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him, you heavens of heavens,
you waters that are above the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them praise Yahweh’s name,
for he commanded, and they were created.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He has also established them forever and ever.
He has made a decree which will not pass away.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Praise Yahweh from the earth,
you great sea creatures, and all depths;

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> lightning and hail, snow and clouds;
stormy wind, fulfilling his word;

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> mountains and all hills;
fruit trees and all cedars;

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> wild animals and all livestock;
small creatures and flying birds;

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> kings of the earth and all peoples;
princes and all judges of the earth;

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> both young men and maidens;
old men and children:

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> let them praise Yahweh’s name,
for his name alone is exalted.
His glory is above the earth and the heavens.

<sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He has lifted up the horn of his people,
the praise of all his saints,
even of the children of Israel, a people near to him.
Praise Yah!
