<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise Yah!
Praise Yahweh, my soul.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> While I live, I will praise Yahweh.
I will sing praises to my God as long as I exist.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Don’t put your trust in princes,
in a son of man in whom there is no help.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> His spirit departs, and he returns to the earth.
In that very day, his thoughts perish.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Happy is he who has the God of Jacob for his help,
whose hope is in Yahweh, his God:

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> who made heaven and earth,
the sea, and all that is in them;
who keeps truth forever;

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> who executes justice for the oppressed;
who gives food to the hungry.
Yahweh frees the prisoners.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahweh opens the eyes of the blind.
Yahweh raises up those who are bowed down.
Yahweh loves the righteous.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Yahweh preserves the foreigners.
He upholds the fatherless and widow,
but he turns the way of the wicked upside down.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Yahweh will reign forever;
your God, O Zion, to all generations.
Praise Yah!
