---
number: 65
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David. A song."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Praise waits for you, God, in Zion.
Vows shall be performed to you.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> You who hear prayer,
all men will come to you.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Sins overwhelmed me,
but you atoned for our transgressions.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Blessed is the one whom you choose and cause to come near,
that he may live in your courts.
We will be filled with the goodness of your house,
your holy temple.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> By awesome deeds of righteousness, you answer us,
God of our salvation.
You who are the hope of all the ends of the earth,
of those who are far away on the sea.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> By your power, you form the mountains,
having armed yourself with strength.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> You still the roaring of the seas,
the roaring of their waves,
and the turmoil of the nations.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They also who dwell in faraway places are afraid at your wonders.
You call the morning’s dawn and the evening with songs of joy.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You visit the earth, and water it.
You greatly enrich it.
The river of God is full of water.
You provide them grain, for so you have ordained it.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You drench its furrows.
You level its ridges.
You soften it with showers.
You bless it with a crop.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> You crown the year with your bounty.
Your carts overflow with abundance.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> The wilderness grasslands overflow.
The hills are clothed with gladness.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> The pastures are covered with flocks.
The valleys also are clothed with grain.
They shout for joy!
They also sing.
