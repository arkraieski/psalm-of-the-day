---
number: 21
title: "For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The king rejoices in your strength, Yahweh!
How greatly he rejoices in your salvation!

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> You have given him his heart’s desire,
and have not withheld the request of his lips.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For you meet him with the blessings of goodness.
You set a crown of fine gold on his head.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He asked life of you and you gave it to him,
even length of days forever and ever.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> His glory is great in your salvation.
You lay honor and majesty on him.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> For you make him most blessed forever.
You make him glad with joy in your presence.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> For the king trusts in Yahweh.
Through the loving kindness of the Most High, he shall not be moved.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Your hand will find out all of your enemies.
Your right hand will find out those who hate you.

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You will make them as a fiery furnace in the time of your anger.
Yahweh will swallow them up in his wrath.
The fire shall devour them.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You will destroy their descendants from the earth,
their posterity from among the children of men.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> For they intended evil against you.
They plotted evil against you which cannot succeed.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> For you will make them turn their back,
when you aim drawn bows at their face.

<sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Be exalted, Yahweh, in your strength,
so we will sing and praise your power.
