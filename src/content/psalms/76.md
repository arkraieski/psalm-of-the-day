---
number: 76
title: "For the Chief Musician. On stringed instruments. A Psalm by Asaph. A song."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> In Judah, God is known.
His name is great in Israel.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> His tabernacle is also in Salem;
His dwelling place in Zion.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> There he broke the flaming arrows of the bow,
the shield, and the sword, and the weapons of war.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Glorious are you, and excellent,
more than mountains of game.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Valiant men lie plundered,
they have slept their last sleep.
None of the men of war can lift their hands.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> At your rebuke, God of Jacob,
both chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> You, even you, are to be feared.
Who can stand in your sight when you are angry?

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You pronounced judgment from heaven.
The earth feared, and was silent,

<sup class="verse-num">9</sup> when God arose to judgment,
to save all the afflicted ones of the earth.

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Surely the wrath of man praises you.
The survivors of your wrath are restrained.

<sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Make vows to Yahweh your God, and fulfill them!
Let all of his neighbors bring presents to him who is to be feared.

<sup class="verse-num">12</sup> He will cut off the spirit of princes.
He is feared by the kings of the earth.
