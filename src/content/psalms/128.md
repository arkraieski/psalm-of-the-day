---
number: 128
title: "A Song of Ascents."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blessed is everyone who fears Yahweh,
who walks in his ways.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> For you will eat the labor of your hands.
You will be happy, and it will be well with you.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Your wife will be as a fruitful vine in the innermost parts of your house,
your children like olive plants around your table.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Behold, thus is the man blessed who fears Yahweh.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> May Yahweh bless you out of Zion,
and may you see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Yes, may you see your children’s children.
Peace be upon Israel.
