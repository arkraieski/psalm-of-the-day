---
number: 61
title: "For the Chief Musician. For a stringed instrument. By David."
testament: "OT"
---

<sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hear my cry, God.
Listen to my prayer.

<sup class="verse-num">2</sup> From the end of the earth, I will call to you when my heart is overwhelmed.
Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

<sup class="verse-num">3</sup> For you have been a refuge for me,
a strong tower from the enemy.

<sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I will dwell in your tent forever.
I will take refuge in the shelter of your wings.

<sup class="verse-num">5</sup> For you, God, have heard my vows.
You have given me the heritage of those who fear your name.

<sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You will prolong the king’s life.
His years will be for generations.

<sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He shall be enthroned in God’s presence forever.
Appoint your loving kindness and truth, that they may preserve him.

<sup class="verse-num">8</sup> So I will sing praise to your name forever,
that I may fulfill my vows daily.
